Greater Wisconsin Amanita

A centralized resource for identifying Amanita species in the upper midwestern region of the United States.

This is not an exhaustive list and represents only 33 "species" found over the course of two collecting seasons.

How to Use the Application

The site is organized to help you take your morphological observations of Amanita to the next level.

  • Learn: Use our morphology primer to know what traits to look for out in the field.
  • Identify: Enter a plethora of morphological traits into our identification tool to narrow species candidates.
  • Reference: Access species pages to browse brief description, images, and location information. Use the iNatualrist and mushroom observer links to compare with other observations.
