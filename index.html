Greater Wisconsin Amanita

Hello and welcome! This is the Greater Wisconsin Amanita Identification Tool, created by Mike Hensley and Jason Raiti. We built it to help mushroom enthusiasts move beyond guesses and explore Amanita species through the real traits mycologists use in the field.

How to Use the Application

The site is organized to help you move from morphological trait-based search to local observations and confirmation based on rigorous scientific data from the Greater Wisconsin Amanita project.

  • Identify!: Enter morphological traits to narrow species candidates.
  • Cross Reference: Compare multiple Amanita species using previous observations for visual confirmation.
  • Species List: Browse species descriptions and species details as the project grows and more data becomes available.

Start with the Identify! page and your morphological observations to find potential matches, then optionally use Cross Reference to confirm your identifications with nearby Wisconsin observations.
