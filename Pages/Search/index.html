Shape terms

Convex means rounded outward. Plano-convex means mostly flat but still rounded. Applanate means flat. Campanulate means bell-shaped.

Striations

Look for fine lines or grooves near the cap margin.

What are striations?

Striations are fine radial lines at the cap margin, often caused by the gills showing through thin cap tissue.

Universal veil remnants on cap

Look for warts, patches, powder, or other veil material on the cap surface.

What is the universal veil?

The universal veil is tissue that encloses young Amanita mushrooms. Remnants may remain as warts or patches on the cap.
